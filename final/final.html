Central Perk

This LEGO set features the Central Perk coffee house in the iconic TV show Friends, which is a popular sitcom that ran for 10 years. The show describes the lives of six young people in New York city, who loved and supported each other along the way.

Click on each character.

10 seasons condensed into a miniature coffee house.

Among the six main characters in the show, I feel a strong connection with Monica. Although not as severe as her OCD, I do tend to keep things organized in a specific order as well. Having a younger brother, I frequently find myself understanding her feelings while watching the show.

One of the items on my bucket list is to visit the Warner Bros Studio in Hollywood and spots in New York that are featured in the show.
